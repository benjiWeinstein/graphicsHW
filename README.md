## Toggles
1. Click 'w' to toggle wireframe
2. Click 'h' to toggle helpers
3. Click '1' to toggle x-axis rotation
4. Click '2' to toggle z-axis rotation
5. Click 'o' to toggle orbit controls
6. Click 's' to toggle stars
